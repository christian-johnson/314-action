{\it The November 16 version of H.R. 1, the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, that passed the House would substantially increase the tax burden on graduate students receiving a tuition remission. Here we provide a list of members of Congress representing districts that contain universities with high amounts of research activity who nevertheless voted in favor of H.R. 1. Because they represent a constituency which will be negatively impacted by this bill, we believe these members of Congress are most likely to be amenable to removing the relevant provision.}
